Outer Wilds (2019)

I played this videogame in 2022 and it immediately become my favourite one.

As a guy obsessed by space, stars and galaxies this game is a gift from gods.

I care so much about my experience with Outer Wilds that I don't even want to talk about it to not spoil anything to my friends.

Just play it.

A Short Hike (2020)

If you want to play a relaxing, mind-healing and hearth-touching game this is the one for you.

A beautiful hiking experience will guide you thru an exciting challenge about self-consciousness, finding our own path in life and caring about other people.

Mix these emotions with a simple but effective flying gameplay to find every secrets on the map and what you a have is a must-play videogame.

Destiny 2 (2017)

This game was my salvation (*wink*) during the first pandemic's lockdown.

I made so much friends playing Destiny 2 that I don't even consider it a game anymore.

It become a social space where we can talk about our days, problems and dreams.

Sometimes (when personal commitments allow us) we also play one of the best multiplayer experience you can have: Raids.

Elden Ring (2022)

As a souls-like enthusiast, this game is a master-piece.

Dark Souls in an open world. Miyazaki did (almost) everything right to create an experience that you are going to remember for years.

An absurd amount of armor, weapons, enchantments, miracles and items will make every run a unique adventure.

"All that glisters is not gold". I would have liked a higher difficulty in NG+ (and above) apart from bosses' output damage.

Portal (2007) / Portal 2 (2011)

Well, it's the Portal Saga + Portal 3 was the friends we made along the way.

Shoot here, shoot there, jump and don't trust the cake. Level completed.

In both games you have an amazing storyline to follow, but in Portal 2 you have two players co-op mode.

Yes, this means you can relocate the exit portal to your mate. Funniest s**t I've ever done.

I'm planning on catching up on all the old games (but also recent ones) that I've never played but wanted to, so I'm hoping to add more to this list.
