Since I was a child I've always liked travelling around the world (mostly Europe).

I took my first flight when I was 7 months old. It was the flight to come home.

I was born in Russia in July 1998 and I was adopted by an Italian family in early 1999.

I don't remember anything about Russia but I have an amazing movie that my parents recorded when they come to Russia.

From that moment, my family and I have always travelled every summer to a different place.

My favourite ones have been to go to Balearic Islands (three different islands in three different times), Greece and driving around Umbria.

I also travel a lot with my friends.

With my female best friend we went to Berlin, near Barcelona and London (we went on a study trip).

With two of my high school best friends we went to Dublin to celebrate the end of 2022.

It was one of the best trip I've ever did. We saw almost everything in the city and we never felt tired.

But, my favourite trip was the one when I went to the USA. Los Angeles and San Francisco!

It was my first time outside the EU and discovering another world was really exciting.

I'm not planning to go back because I would like to visit other US cities but it certainly worths another trip.

In my future plan I see my first Asian country: Japan.

I would like to go there in Spring 2024 with my Dublin friends and other bunch of people.
